About

Streets of Food began as a simple passion project among a group of friends who loved to travel and explore street food. What started as a photo album to share our culinary adventures quickly evolved into a mission to document and share the most authentic street food experiences from around the world.

From the night markets of Malaysia to the food trucks of Mexico City, we've captured hundreds of dishes, always focusing on authenticity, flavor, and the stories behind the food.

Our mission is to preserve and promote street food culture across the globe. We believe that some of the most genuine culinary experiences don't happen in high-end restaurants but on bustling street corners, where recipes passed down through generations tell the story of a culture.
